Discovery as you are aware is the action or process of discovering or being discovered.

The Need for Data Discovery

Traditional BI or Data Analysis answers the “what” of the information and gives us insights into what happened, but does not answer “why” something happened like “why did the usage of product decreased” or “why does product feature adoption went down”. Data discovery is the technology that helps users in answering the question beginning with “why”.

Goals

  • Understanding Business Trends
  • Assessment of business assumptions and strategies
  • Making Business Proactive from Reactive
  • Business Attributes to be monitored in order to Mitigate the risk
  • Finding independent elements which are affecting final outcomes.
  • Actionable insights with intensity of correlation, thresholds and importance of affecting business.

Basically Data Discovery improves decision-making process, powers growth strategies, significantly boosts the customer experience

Data Discovery Process

  • Identifying Pain Points
  • Diverse Data Collection
  • Data Preparation
  • Model Building
  • Storytelling based on insights discovered
